Dogs




Signs Your Dog Needs to go to the Vet

  • If your dog goes more than one day without eating.
  • if your dog is vommiting/has diarrhea consistently for 8 hours or longer.
  • If your dog is having breathing difficulties (difficulty breathing, making weird noises when breathing, etc.).
  • If your dog is suddenly drinking a lot more water than is usual.
  • Sudden weight loss, usually 10% of their overall body weight or more.
  • If your dog is suddenly more lethargic and continues to be so for two days or longer.
  • If your dog has suddenly aggressive behavior and you can't determine a reason.
