Cats




Signs Your Cat Needs to go to the Vet

  • If your cat has a sudden change of behavior/ seems in distress (i.e. howling, hiding, crying, etc.)
  • If your cats literbox habits change, usually urinating outside of the box or seeming unable to use the box when they try.
  • If your cat is vomiting on a regular basis.
  • If your cat is suddenly lethargic to the point of disinterest in their food/play for longer than two days.
  • If your cats appetite changes noticeably, either eating much more or much less than usual.
  • If your cat is dragging their back legs.
  • If you feel an unusual lump or growth anywhere on your cat.
  • If you notice your cat coughing or having trouble breathing.
  • After any fight with another cat or other animal.
